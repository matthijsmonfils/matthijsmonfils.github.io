    In my third year at the AUAS, where i follow Computer Technology & Computer Science (mainly specialized in Business IT & Management), I followed the course Dark Tech.
    which focusses on the darker side of technology and the responsibility you carry when creating any form of Technology. Within this course i specialized in a certain role, the creative Technologist.
    which mainly focusses on bringing together Design and Technology.
    During this course i was assigned to do a project for the City of Amsterdam, for whom i, along with four other students had to create a provocatype (a provocing prototatype). The subject this provocatype had the based on was Data & Location-Tracking, a very sensitive topic, with which a lot of people, especially teenagers and adolescents, have a lot of experience.

    Jachtseizoen - the Data Hunt

    During the creation of our Provocatype we came up with the idea to create a masked application. Where the user uses the application for a different reason than the actual intended purpose of the application. We masked it as a mobile game called 'Jachtseizoen - The Data Hunt', a game where you can catch your friends based on their tracking location and powerups. Based on the popular TV show 'Jachtseizoen' (Hunting Season). But in reality the actual purpose of our application was to get the location data of the user to sell to third parties. After the user has played the game for a while we reveal the true identity of the application, which should create awareness about Data & Location-Tracking, as well as how developers can use these types of Dark Patterns to mislead users.
